Wildlondon /// 130 × 210 // 2025
The aim of this book is to experience London from the perspective of its animals: how do they perceive the city? What do they pay attention to, which senses guide their perception, and what risks do they face? Through essays and dialogues, we gain a deeper insight into their point of view. This publication was made possible by the collective image and text material gathered by the entire class. A detailed exploration of twenty-two animals is presented both textually and visually—a blend of interpretation, research, and imagination.
